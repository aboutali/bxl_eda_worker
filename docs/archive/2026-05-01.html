A 90-minute Trump–Putin call on Ukraine dominated the day, with Moscow projecting confidence and Washington signaling optimism on a settlement, even as Ukrainian strikes on the Tuapse oil hub drew nuclear-tinged rhetoric from a Kremlin-linked oligarch and the EU reiterated its Ukraine position and pushed to keep Russia out of international sports and cultural fora — alignment points Bern will need to track.
