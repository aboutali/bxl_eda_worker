Today

Brussels approved its heaviest sanctions tranche of the year, listing 74 vessels and 18 entities for oil price-cap evasion and Yamal LNG re-exports, alongside a parallel update to dual-use Regulation 2021/821 adding 32 emerging-tech entries and a catch-all clause; Bern's EDA has signalled broad alignment, though SECO remains locked in talks with Geneva commodity traders over the reach of the LNG provisions, and a Commission taskforce has flagged two unnamed Zurich institutes for suspect correspondent links.
